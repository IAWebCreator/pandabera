HOW TO BUY

Step 1: Get a Wallet - Download a reputable Ethereum wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet.

Step 2: Acquire ETH - Purchase Ethereum (ETH) from a cryptocurrency exchange like Coinbase or Binance.

Step 3: Connect to a DEX - Connect your wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap or SushiSwap.

Step 4: Swap ETH for PBR - Use the DEX's swap feature to exchange your ETH for PANDABERA (PBR).
